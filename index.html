   About Me

 

Welcome to my virtual abode, fellow Internaut!

Hello there! My name is Shail.

I'm a current undergrad Electrical Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. I will be graduating in Spring 2018.

My passion for Engineering began at an early age; As a child, I found myself spending long hours constantly breaking things to see what was happening on the insides and then fixing whatever I had broken. I like to get my hands dirty with Electronics and Software projects. I like understanding why/how a particular system works and figuring out how I can potentially change something to to improve functionality.

TV Shows and Movies

Whether its the latest Marvel movie or the legal Drama Suits, I'm a huge fan.

Hardware Prototyping

Arduino, RaspberryPi... you name it! From schematics to Board Layout to PCB assembly, I love this stuff.

Embedded Software

Be it programming Microcontrollers or hacking the Bluetooth protocol stack

Electric Car Technology

I fancy cars, and I fancy working on them even more; particularly those that run off Li-ion battery packs
